Our Services

Road Accident Fund Claims

We assist victims of road accidents in claiming rightful compensation for injuries, medical costs, and loss of income from the Road Accident Fund (RAF).

Unlawful Arrest Claims

If you've been wrongfully arrested or detained without cause, we help you seek legal redress and compensation through civil claims against the responsible authorities.

Bail Applications

Our legal team provides prompt and effective representation in securing bail, ensuring your rights are protected throughout the legal process.

Divorce Matters

We offer compassionate and strategic guidance through divorce proceedings, including maintenance, custody, and asset division issues.

Civil Litigation

Our firm handles a wide range of civil disputes, representing clients in contractual, property, and personal injury matters in both magistrate and high courts.

Wills and Estate

We assist with drafting wills, setting up trusts, and managing estate planning to ensure your assets are protected and distributed according to your wishes.

Immigration

Whether applying for visas, permanent residency, or resolving immigration disputes, our team guides individuals and families through every step of the process.

Eviction

From defending tenants facing unlawful eviction to assisting landlords with legal procedures, our team provides clear guidance and representation to ensure fair outcomes and compliance with housing laws.
